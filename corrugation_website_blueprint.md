## 4. Products & Capabilities Page
*This section provides technical verification for procurement managers who need to know your material specifications and formatting limits.*

* **Headline:** Precision-Engineered Corrugated Solutions
* **Sub-headline:** From robust transit master cartons to high-definition printed packaging, our dual auto and semi-auto lines deliver structural integrity at scale.

### A. Structural Board Configurations
* **3-Ply Corrugated Cartons (Single Wall):** Ideal for lightweight to medium-weight products, e-commerce shipping, and inner product packaging. Offers excellent flexibility and maximum cost-effectiveness.
* **5-Ply Master Cartons (Double Wall):** Our flagship heavy-duty industrial solution. Engineered for heavy consumer goods, FMCG bulk shipping, and high-stacking stability in warehouses or marine shipping containers.
* **7-Ply Premium Cartons (Triple Wall):** Built for ultimate structural strength. Best suited for heavy machinery parts, automotive components, and international export logistics requiring maximum puncture and impact resistance.

### B. Specialized Master Carton Configurations
* **Universal Slotted Cartons (RSC):** The industry standard for efficient, high-volume shipping. Highly cost-effective and perfectly optimized for our automated high-speed runs.
* **Die-Cut & Custom Cartons:** Precision-cut packaging with specialized hand-holes, ventilation slots, or unique locking tab mechanisms tailored exactly to your product dimensions.
* **Top & Bottom (Telescopic) Boxes:** Ideal for large textiles, garments, and flat-packed items that require easy unboxing and reliable edge protection.

### C. Large-Format Printing & Branding
* **Max-Size Flexographic Printing:** Crisp, high-contrast corporate branding, handling symbols, and tracking graphics printed directly onto large-format master cartons.
* **High-Definition Structural Markings:** Sharp, smudge-resistant ink printing ensuring that your tracking codes, safety warnings, and logistical data remain scannable throughout the entire automated supply chain.
