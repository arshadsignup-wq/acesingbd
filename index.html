Technical Specifications

Corrugated box configurations and material grades

As a specialized corrugated box manufacturer in Bangladesh, our production lines are calibrated for 3 ply, 5 ply, and 7 ply corrugated board configurations. We optimize every run for material efficiency and consistent carton box quality.

“We do not guess strength. We verify it. Every batch of corrugated boxes is tested to ensure your products are fully protected during shipping and storage.”

3-Ply Single WallLightweight

Ideal for e-commerce packaging, inner carton liners, and lightweight product shipping. Offers excellent flexibility with lower material cost per unit, making it the preferred choice for high-volume retail and online orders.

5-Ply Double WallHeavy-Duty

Engineered for FMCG bulk shipping, consumer goods, and warehouse stacking. Double wall construction provides superior compression strength and moisture resistance for long-distance transport and container loading.

7-Ply Triple WallMaximum

Built for heavy machinery parts, automotive components, and international export cargo. Triple wall corrugated board delivers maximum burst strength and edge crush resistance to protect high-value goods during overseas shipment.

Universal Slotted (RSC)Standard

The industry standard regular slotted container for high-volume automated packing lines. Offers efficient material usage, easy assembly, and compatibility with all sealing methods including tape, glue, and staple closure.

Flexographic PrintingBranding

Up to 4-color flexographic printing for corporate branding, product labeling, barcodes, and handling instructions. Smudge-resistant inks ensure scannable tracking data throughout the entire supply chain.
